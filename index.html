Hello and welcome!

This site is meant to highlight the gender gap in the STEM field and demonstrate how it impacts women on an individual scale. If you click on the survey tab above, you may share your own story about the experiences and/or struggles you have had as a woman in STEM in the form provided. Or, if you would like access to interesting statistics and resources to help fight sexism in the workforce, hop on over to the Info tab.
